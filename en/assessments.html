Compliance Assessment

Web Standards Centre of Expertise (WS CoE) performs compliance assessments on the department's public facing web assets.

When the team receives an assessment request, through the NC-NW-WS-GD mailbox, an assessment workbook will be sent to you for your completion. Each assessment is based on the asset assessment workbook that the asset owner is responsible for maintaining. This document is used for each assessment, so before submitting a re-assessment request, review the documentation to ensure that the correct information is available for us to perform an assessment.

The Web Standards CoE will perform an assessment to ensure that all requirements comply with the web standard(s). The results will identify outstanding items that will need to be resolved. It is the responsibility of each application developer and content author to review their site to ensure that all instances of the failed-checkpoints are resolved prior to submitting a re-assessment request. Each time a re-assessment is performed, a new random sample of pages will be assessed. Once all outstanding items have been resolved, an email notification will be sent out that your asset met 100% Web Conformance.

Compliance Waiver

Under extenuating circumstances a website or web-based application may need to release into a production environment without meeting web standards compliance. On such rare occasions, the content authorities/development groups may submit a compliance waiver request to the Web Standards Centre of Expertise. Should WS CoE concur that no critical accessibility requirements have been compromised; the content authorities/development groups may seek their DG's written approval to proceed.

You can reach us by email at NC-NW-WS-GD.
